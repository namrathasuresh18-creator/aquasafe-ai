Environmental Awareness Center

Learn how chemical parameters alter fragile riverbeds and marine lifespans.

Dissolved Oxygen (DO)

Waterways need dissolved oxygen to support respiratory fish gills. Levels dropping below 2.0 mg/L create hypoxic biological dead zones, killing aquatic flora and fauna.

Ask AI Explainer →

pH Acidic Corrosions

Ideal pH rests between 6.5 and 8.5. Run-off from mining or heavy chemical dumpings decreases pH below 6.0, accelerating the dissolution of toxic heavy metals directly into fish tissue.

Ask AI Explainer →

Turbidity Suspensions

Turbid cloudy water blocks solar arrays, completely halting underwater plant photosynthesis. Suspended mud dust acts as solar collectors, increasing water temperatures to stressed margins.
